Things to Do

Bookstore and Information Center

Our Camp Bookstore has the area’s largest selection of books on Spiritualism, spirituality and metaphysics. We also carry a large selection of CDs, DVD's, art from local artisans, greeting cards, divination cards, crystals, stones, jewelry, statues, Native American crafts and other unique gift items.
As the Information Center, we are always happy to answer questions about the Camp and/or special order books/items upon request. (There will be a shipping and handling charge on mail orders). Direct your requests to CassadagaCampBookstore@gmail.com or call (386) 228-2880.
The Spiritualist Camp Welcome Center is also located inside the bookstore. Here you will find current literature on a myriad of Camp activities. An ATM is also available for your convenience.
There is a whiteboard in the Welcome Center where some of our certified mediums and healers sign in daily to accept walk-in appointments. However, their schedules fill up fast. It is highly recommended that you browse our Mediums and Healers Lists on this website and make your appointment in advance of your visit.
Store hours: Monday - Saturday 10:am to 6:pm and Sunday 11:30 am to 5:pm Mailing address: P.O. Box 319, Cassadaga, FL 32706.

Parks

There are 7 parks and meditation gardens scattered throughout the community giving visitors a place to enjoy the peace and serenity that is Cassadaga. Each park is unique and offers an opportunity to connect with nature and your higher self. Discover which park resonates with you.

Historical Tours

Historical Tours every Friday & Saturday at 2PM. Night Tours are available Saturday evenings at 7:30 pm.

  • Historical Tour

    • Fridays and Saturdays at 2PM A guided tour through this Victorian era community. Learn about Cassadaga's historic, unique and mysterious beginnings and the spirit activity that still goes on in the historical homes of present day mediums. The tour is a great way to gain insight into Spiritualism and to feel the wonderful energy that is Cassadaga. The cost is $15 per Adult, $7.50 per Child 7-12 and Children 6 or under are FREE.

  • Encounter the Spirit Night Tour

    • Saturdays at 7:30 pm A guided tour that will walk you through energy "hot spots", enabling you to capture spirit energy that can manifest in any number of ways. Learn about Spiritualism, Mediumship, Physical Phenomena and more. Bring a digital camera to capture images of energy orbs and other spirit manifestation. Although cell phones will work, digital cameras are recommended. The tour includes a walk through of this Victorian era community and entry to 2 of our historic buildings. All questions are welcome and photographs may be taken at any time during the tour. Tours start at the Cassadaga Bookstore and last approximately two hours. Tickets may be purchased at the door, in advance at the Cassadaga Bookstore & Welcome Center or by clicking below. . The cost is $25.00 per Adult, $15.00 per Child 12 and under. Please arrive by 7:15 for check in. (Closed shoes are recommended) This tour is NOT wheelchair accessible. Please refrain from alcohol consumption prior to the tour.

    • Individual Historic and Night Tours can be scheduled for groups of 10 or more with advance notice. ​
